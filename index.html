My Man, Gary

Gary AI

The purpose of this site is an attempt to replicate the perspective of a real person by using state of the art machine learning models. It is also an attempt to scale up Gary Vaynerchuk (a.k.a. Gary Vee), which brings massive value to the community.

The attempt to replicate Gary Vee way of thinking, all models were trained based on hundreds of hours of his publicly available videos, talks, and podcasts. All of the sample quotes below were generated by using machine learning and not edited by human to correct them. With one exception: some swear words were replaced to make it more accessible.

Some of the samples might not make much sense or are funny. Some can provide scarily deep insights that are aligned with the vision Gary is sharing with the world.

This site is not affiliated with Gary Vaynerchuk and does not represent his official statements. The creation of this site was for education, fun, and entertainment only.

Enjoy!
